About This Project

Exposure Management Reference is a concise, practical reference covering modern threat and vulnerability management concepts, cloud security, application security, and emerging domains such as AI security.

The content is intended as a quick-reference and learning aid for practitioners, architects, and security leaders. Concepts reflect commonly used industry terminology and frameworks.

Content was curated by a human author with support from AI assistants (e.g., Microsoft Copilot and Claude).
